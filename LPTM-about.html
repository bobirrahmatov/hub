Loan Product & Transaction Management (LPTM) Standup was completed globally in 2023 which through the power of partnerships with our product owners and business leaders we were able to design the seamless and robust LPTM process that has upgraded the way we support Wealth Lending Products (Loans and LCs) by instituting an improved lending experience, increased control throughout the deal lifecycle, and simplification of processes.

LPTM Consists of 4 Important Functions

LPTM delivers a simplified, transparent, and controlled approach to supporting Wealth Lending Products across the full deal lifecycle — empowering teams, improving client experience, and strengthening operational oversight.

Credit Process Management

Is responsible for supporting multiple aspects of the credit approval process, specifically by ensuring quality of captured credit data, KYC compliance, and credit policies/standards and regulatory requirements are adhered to and addressed appropriately.

Deal Closing, Booking and Funding

Covers all Wealth Lending Products (Loans and LCs) for closing (Margin Lending A1 and Partner Lending Classifiably Managed deals will not go through the closing process) and will perform operational feasibility checks, compile the loan closing package, and conduct weekly forums with relevant stakeholders.

Loan & Facility Management

Increases accountability and applies strategic direction to Loan Lifecycle Management. Drives the Loan Credit Reconciliation and Monitoring, addresses non-credit inquiries, manages client's escalations, assists in payoff coordination's, special handling, and handles billing issues for a more seamless client experience.

Support Functions – Data & Reporting

Implements governance on Data Definition and Data Capture Process. Establishes Processes & Controls for accurate Regulatory Reporting and enhances monitoring mechanisms for pro-actively identifying data quality issues.
